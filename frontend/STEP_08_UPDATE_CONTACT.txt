---------------------

1. User fills out form (name, email, subject, message, phone)
2. User clicks "Send Message"
3. Form validates (browser validation)
4. handleSubmit calls contactService.submitContact()
5. Service makes POST request to /api/contact
6. Backend validates and saves contact
7. Response returned to frontend
8. Success: Show success message, clear form
9. Error: Display error message
